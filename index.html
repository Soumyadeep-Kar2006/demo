Our Heritage

From Kolkata's Historic Tanneries to the World.

At SKINOWEAR®, we believe that leather is more than a material—it is a legacy of craftsmanship, character, and timeless style. Rooted in the rich leathercraft traditions of Kolkata, India, our atelier combines generations of artisanal expertise with contemporary luxury and dark-fashion aesthetics.

From leather jackets, vests, and bags to wallets, belts, accessories, and bespoke leather goods, every piece is created with an uncompromising commitment to quality. Each hide is hand-selected, every stitch meticulously placed, and every hardware element carefully chosen to ensure durability, functionality, and elegance.

We don't simply manufacture leather products; we craft enduring companions designed to age beautifully, develop a unique patina, and tell your story over time. Every SKINOWEAR® creation embodies authenticity, individuality, and the spirit of true craftsmanship.

Built for those who appreciate timeless design and exceptional quality, SKINOWEAR® stands as a symbol of leather artistry, heritage, and modern sophistication.
