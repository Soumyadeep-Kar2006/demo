Our Heritage

A Legacy Reimagined Through Leather.

SKINOWEAR was born from a world we have known all our lives. Our family spent generations in the leather trade, working closely with hides, tanneries, and the craftsmen whose hands kept the tradition alive. Leather was never something we discovered—it was something we inherited.

Yet our vision was different. While our roots lay in the raw leather industry, we aspired to create something more refined—something that celebrated craftsmanship, character, and timeless design. Rather than following the traditional path, we chose to transform a legacy into a modern expression of luxury.

Every SKINOWEAR creation reflects that journey. From handcrafted jackets and bags to wallets, belts, and bespoke pieces, each product is shaped using carefully selected leather, skilled craftsmanship, and an unwavering attention to detail. We believe true luxury is defined not by excess, but by authenticity, quality, and enduring value.

Leather is one of the few materials that becomes more beautiful with time. It develops character, tells stories, and carries the marks of the life lived alongside it. Our creations are designed to age gracefully, becoming personal companions that grow richer with every journey.

Rooted in generations of leather knowledge and guided by a contemporary vision, SKINOWEAR® stands as a tribute to timeless craftsmanship—where heritage, authenticity, and modern luxury come together without compromise.
